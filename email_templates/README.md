# Email Templates - ReviewSignal Hedge Fund Outreach

## Sequence Overview

4-email sequence for hedge fund outreach via Instantly.

### Timing
- Email 1: Day 0 (Initial outreach)
- Email 2: Day 3 (Case study follow-up)
- Email 3: Day 7 (Demo offer)
- Email 4: Day 14 (Breakup email)

### Variables to Replace

All emails use these variables (replaced by Instantly):
- `{{first_name}}` - Contact first name
- `{{company}}` - Company name
- `{{chain}}` - Example chain (can be personalized)

### Personalization Strategy

**High-value personalization (manual):**
- Check if they cover specific retail chains
- Reference recent fund performance/news
- Mention specific portfolio companies

**Auto-personalization (Instantly):**
- First name
- Company name
- Title
- Industry

### Setup in Instantly

1. **Create Campaign:**
   - Name: "ReviewSignal - Hedge Funds Q1 2026"
   - Type: Cold outreach sequence

2. **Import Templates:**
   - Copy each email content
   - Set delays: 0d, 3d, 7d, 14d
   - Enable tracking (opens, clicks)

3. **Configure Variables:**
   - Map {{first_name}} to Instantly field
   - Map {{company}} to Instantly field
   - Set default values for missing fields

4. **Schedule:**
   - Mon-Fri only
   - 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM EST
   - Max 20 emails/day per domain

### Performance Targets

| Metric | Target | Good | Excellent |
|--------|--------|------|-----------|
| Open rate | 30% | 40% | 50%+ |
| Reply rate | 5% | 8% | 12%+ |
| Demo booking | 2% | 4% | 6%+ |
| Conversion | 0.5% | 1% | 2%+ |

**With 1,000 leads:**
- Expected demos: 20-40
- Expected pilots: 5-10
- Expected customers: 5 (target)

### A/B Testing

**Test variations:**
- Subject lines (data-driven vs curiosity)
- Opening line (problem vs opportunity)
- CTA (demo vs trial vs case study)
- Length (short vs detailed)

### Compliance

**Legal requirements:**
- ✅ Include unsubscribe link (Instantly handles)
- ✅ Real physical address (add to signature)
- ✅ Honest subject lines (no spam words)
- ✅ CAN-SPAM compliant

**Best practices:**
- Don't mention "urgent" or "limited time" (spam triggers)
- Keep under 200 words per email
- One clear CTA per email
- Professional tone (B2B financial)

### Optimization

**Weekly review:**
- Check open rates by subject line
- Analyze reply sentiment
- Track demo booking rate
- Monitor unsubscribe rate

**Monthly review:**
- A/B test results
- Sequence timing optimization
- Template refinement
- ROI calculation
